Trézor Bridge® is the essential software layer that allows your Trezor® hardware wallet to communicate directly with your browser. Whether you're managing your cryptocurrency securely or interacting with dApps, Trézor Bridge™ ensures smooth, encrypted communication between your device and supported interfaces.

Designed for users who prioritize security and usability, Trézor Bridge® eliminates the need for browser extensions, reducing the risk of third-party vulnerabilities. This lightweight background application runs seamlessly and provides plug-and-play connectivity for supported platforms. Regular updates and open-source transparency make it a trusted bridge between your offline wallet and the online world.

With support for Windows, macOS, and Linux, Trézor Bridge® is easy to install and even easier to trust. Built by the team behind the world’s first hardware wallet, it brings together innovation, reliability, and peace of mind — all in one package.
